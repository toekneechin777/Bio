Foraging Swarm Robotics

Currently conducting research in Swarm Robotics under PhD student John Harwell and Professor Maria Gini. The focus of the research is maximizing energy efficiency in large swarms of small robots in various environmental scenarios.
A simulation is coded up with the new swarm robotics strategy through a program called ARGoS.
Paper has been submitted to the AAMAS 2019 Conference.
